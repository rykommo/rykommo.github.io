Kanto Story/Region Guide

PALLET TOWN

✓▾
  • Go down the stairs and exit the house.
  • Go north to the grass.
  • Pick your starter pokemon and go south.
  • Fight your rival and exit the house.
  • Go north to route 1.

ROUTE 1

✓▾
  • Go north to viridian city.

VIRIDIAN CITY

✓▾

  • You will get the oak's parcel.
  • Exit the house and get your Pokemon.
  • Set TP location.
  • Go south to route 1.
  • Go south to pallet town.

  • Go north to route 2.
  • Go north to pewter city.

PEWTER CITY

✓▾

  • Exit the gym and go back north to cerulean city.
