GIRATINA FIGHT

✓▾
  • Swap Graveler to 2nd position, Pory to 3rd position, then put a weak mon in first.
  • Go north and fight the legendary pokemon. This fight is very random so in case of lose just re-enter the dimension and select yes to get back to the fight.
  • Turn 1: Use stealth rock and nasty plot (can use X Sp. Def if you want).
  • Turn 2: If needed, heal pory status effects or HP, use Shadow Ball.
  • In case of Shadow Force, focus the ??? on Pory’s (right) side first.
  • If any Pokemon goes down, swap in another Shadow Ball user. If Giratina stalls for a turn, heal. Try to win the 28.9% damage cap that way.
  • Click on the white portal.
  • Say yes to exit the area. Fly to sandgem town.

POKEMON LEAGUE

✓▾
  • Go north and go up the waterfall. Go in the building north.
  • Make sure you have a couple of Chople Berries, potions and revives.
  • Go north all the way near the guy with the green hair and you will fight your rival without setup.

  • Bug: If Scizor leads, use your fire type first. If Yanmega leads, use Ice Beam before nasty plot.

  • For the rest hope for no status effects. Else, swap and heal/revive.
