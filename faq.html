Bounty System Guide

Bounty Status

Indicates if the hunt is active. Only Open bounties can be claimed for prizes.

✨ The Target

The image and title define what species you need. Sub-text clarifies specific constraints.

👤 The Issuer

The member paying the prize. Whisper them in-game or tag them in Discord to claim.

💰 The Prize

The guaranteed reward for a successful capture. (Yen or RP Vouchers).

⚠

01 The Golden Rule

To preserve the competitive spirit of Team AïR, the Pokémon MUST be your own Original Trainer (OT). Bounties reward active effort, not wealth.

▶ OT must match your character.
▶ Date must be AFTER bounty post.
Core Mechanics +
  • Eligibility: Exclusive to active Team AïR members.
  • Submission: Link the Pokémon to the Issuer via Discord or in-game Mail/Whisper.
  • Duration: Bounties stay open until a verified claim is made.
Terminology Guide +
  • Secret Shiny: Rare 1/16th star particle effect on encounter.
  • Dex Gap: A species currently missing from our Team Master Shiny Dex.
  • Lure Rarity: Shinies found via Lure-specific spawns or rates.
First Come, First Served +
Prizes go to the first person who presents a valid catch to the Issuer. If two people catch the same target, the timestamp of the first message to the Issuer determines the winner.
Submitting Multiple Claims +

One catch CAN claim multiple bounties!

If your catch fits multiple open categories (e.g., a "Blue Shiny" that is also a "Unova Dex Gap"), you are eligible for both rewards from their respective Issuers.
