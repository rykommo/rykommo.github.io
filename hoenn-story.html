Hoenn Story/Region Guide

GENERAL TIPS

✓▾
  • This guide can help you finish the Hoenn region in ~1:45h with optimal gameplay!
  • Before starting the run, get a few potions and plenty of repels.
  • If you want, you can get a few revives and hyper potions for late game too.
  • Additionally, you will need up to 2 Chople Berries and a Silk Scarf.
  • For all important story fights, the recommended fight setup is described.
  • Before the 8th badge, only a few fights need any setup.
  • For some fights, a stealth rock setup can increase overall speed or help with consistency in case of crits.
  • In the rare case of crits/poison killing your Pory, feel free to revive and switch back in according to described setup.
  • The default Pory item is Silk Scarf (unless described otherwise).
