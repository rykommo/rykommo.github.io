Story/Region Guide (Speedrun)

Preparation Phase

To efficiently rerun the regions you will need a specialized team:

  • Carry Mon: Porygon Z. An excellent special attacker with enough speed and power to clear all story content.
  • Critical Support: Sudowoodo. Helps clear Gym Leaders and Elite 4 members/Champions who utilize Sturdy and Focus Sashes.
  • Tactics: Sudowoodo sets up Stealth Rock turn one, lives a hit with Sturdy, then uses Sucker Punch for chip damage so Porygon can oneshot everything.
Core Strategy

How to use this interactive guide:

  • Navigation: Click on one of the Regions at the bottom to load the Rerun Guide.
  • Step-by-Step: It shows exactly what to do at every point in the Story.
  • Mark Progress: After finishing an element, click on it to mark it as complete and move on to the next point.
  • See Below: To view each Region guide head to the bottom and click on any Region to start!
Resource Management

Alternatives if Porygon or evolution items are expensive during event seasons:

  • Strong Pivot: Gallade (High coverage and physical power).
  • Moxie Snowball: Gyarados or Krookodile.
  • Multi-Hit: Skill Link Cloyster.
