Unova Story/Region Guide

NUVEMA TOWN

✓▾
  • Click on the box to pick your starter pokemon.
  • Battle Bianca and then battle Cheren.
  • Go down the stairs. Exit the house.
  • Go in the house south west and talk to Bianca. Exit the house.
  • Go north west and talk to Cheren.
  • Enter the laboratory and professor juniper will give you a pokedex after you say yes to her 2nd question.
  • Exit the house and your mom will give you a town map. Go north east to route 1.

ROUTE 1

✓▾
  • Go north and professor juniper will give you x5 pokeball.
  • Go north and talk to Bianca. Say yes and go north to Accumula Town.

ACCUMULA TOWN

✓▾
  • Go east to the pokecenter and talk with professor juniper.
  • Get account mons from PC. Buy some repels.
  • Go west and talk to Cheren. Battle N.
  • Go west to route 2.

ROUTE 2

✓▾
  • Go west and your mom will give you the running shoes. Follow the path.
  • Avoid all trainers possible. Follow the path until you see stairs to your right, go north to battle Bianca.
  • Go north to Striaton City.

STRIATON CITY

✓▾
  • Go in the house north west of the city and talk to Cheren. Beat him. Exit the house.
  • Go east from the pokecenter and enter the gym.
  • Stand on the pokemon type that is super effective to the pokemon type on the wall.
  • Go north and beat the 2 trainers. Beat the gym leader with no setup.
  • Exit the gym and Fennel will give you a HM-Cut. Teach to Bidoof. Exit the house.
  • Go north east of the city to enter Dreamyard.

DREAMYARD

✓▾
  • Cut the tree. Go north to fight the 2 plasma members.
  • Run back to Striaton City.

STRIATON CITY (PART 2)

✓▾
  • Go in the house south of the gym. Go to the 2nd floor and talk to fennel.
  • Exit the house. Go north west of the city to a fountain.
  • Go north to route 3.

ROUTE 3

✓▾
  • Go north. The house north from the twins has a girl that can heal your pokemon if you need PP.
  • Go west to fight Cheren.
  • Go west all the way and talk to Cheren. Say yes and enter the wellspring cave.
  • Talk to Cheren and battle the plasma members.
  • Exit the cave. Go east and talk to Bianca.
  • Go south. Avoid all the trainers.
  • Follow the path and go west and enter Nacrene City.

NACRENE CITY

✓▾
  • Go west and Cheren will give you x3 Chesto berry.
  • Go north east from the pokecenter and enter the gym. You will fight N.
  • Go in the gym. Go north all the way and enter the door.
  • Read the books: bottom left, middle left, top left, bottom right, middle right and go down the stairs.
  • Beat the gym leader with no setup. Exit the gym.
  • Go north west of the city towards Pinwheel Forest.

PINWHEEL FOREST

✓▾
  • Talk to the first person you see on route to get a HM-Rock Smash. Teach to Bidoof.
  • Go into the forest. Go west and follow the path fighting plasma members along the way.
  • Avoid all other trainers. Use repels through grass.
  • After the final plasma member, you will get a dragon skull.
  • Go east. Go north to the bridge gate.

BRIDGE GATE

✓▾
  • Follow the path and go to Castelia City.

CASTELIA CITY

✓▾
  • Go west all the way and go north to the next area. Go to the gym to your right.
  • Go north to the next area. Talk to the guy in green and battle him.
  • Go south from the guy in green on the western side to get in the next area.
  • Go south and talk to the guy in green. Say yes to fight him.
  • Go south to the next area. Go south to the dock area. Go south to the guy in green to battle him.
  • Go north to the next area. Go east and go south to the next area.
  • Talk to the gym leader and go north to the next area.
  • Enter nearest building. Go to 55th floor and battle north east trainer. Get exp. share.
  • Use on Bidoof and Natu to evolve. Exit building. Go west all the way.
  • Then go north to the next area. Go to the building to your left. Talk to the plasma member and say yes.
  • Beat him and enter the building. Go north and talk to Ghetsis.
  • Exit the building and go east to enter the gym.
  • Go north and press a switch. Go east to fight a trainer and press switch.
  • Go north and avoid the first switch that you see. Press the switch north east.
  • Go west and press a switch. Go north east and press a switch. Go west and press a switch.
  • Go south west and avoid the next switch. Go north and press a switch.
  • Go north east to fight a trainer and press the switch.
  • Go north east and beat the gym leader with no setup.
  • Exit the gym and go north to the next area. Go north to the next area.
  • Go north to fight Bianca. Go north to route 4.

ROUTE 4

✓▾
  • Go North to fight Cheren. Go north avoiding all the trainers.
  • Professor Juniper will give you x10 ultra ball.
  • Go north to Nimbasa City.

NIMBASA CITY

✓▾
  • Go north to fight a plasma member. You will get a bicycle.
  • Go to the house north west of PC. Get HM04-Strength from the north west person.
  • Teach to Geodude. Exit the house and go east to the next area. Go east and battle N.
  • Go west and enter the gym.
  • Go in the car to your right. Battle the trainer and hit the switch. Enter the car.
  • Avoid the trainer and hit the switch. Enter the car north.
  • Hit the switch and enter the car to your right.
  • Beat the gym leader with no setup. Use teleporter on right.
  • Exit the gym. Go west out of the gate.
  • Go north and talk to Bianca at the musical theatre. Exit theatre.
  • Go west to route 5.

ROUTE 5

✓▾
  • Go west and battle Cheren. Go west to driftveil city.

DRIFTVEIL CITY & COLD STORAGE

✓▾
  • Enter PC to set TP location.
  • Go south west to cold storage. Avoid all the trainers.
  • Go south west and enter the building.
  • Slide north from any except western most side. Slide east.
  • Go east avoiding the trainer. Slide east. Go up stairs.
  • Avoid another trainer. Slide east, north, west, south.
  • Avoid the next trainer. Follow the path and avoid the trainer. Slide north and avoid the trainer.
  • Go west. Enter the cargo and fight the 4 rightmost plasma members. Exit the cargo.
  • Go north west up stairs and go south. Exit the building. TP back to PC.
  • Go north east from the pokecenter to enter the gym.
  • Get on the elevator and go down. Go east to fight a trainer. Use the elevator to go down.
  • Go north all the way. Go west all the way. Go south and fight a trainer. Use the elevator to go down.
  • Use the north west elevator to go up. Fight a trainer and use the next elevator to go down.
  • Beat the gym leader with a nasty plot setup.
  • Use the teleporter to go up. Exit the gym.
  • Go north west of the city to battle Bianca. You will get HM-Fly teach to Xatu.
  • Go west to route 6.

ROUTE 6 & CHARGESTONE CAVE

✓▾
  • Go west and follow the path. Go north while avoiding the trainers.
  • Keep north and click on the spider web blocking the cave. Enter the cave.
  • Go north and talk to N. Continue north to talk to Professor Juniper.
  • Push the crystal west. Go north and push the crystal east.
  • Go north then east, push the northern crystal west. Push other crystal south.
  • Go south and cross the bridge. Push the crystal south. Go down the stairs.
  • Go west and talk to the twins to get x2 nugget. Go up the stairs.
  • Go north and fight the plasma members. Push crystals west and north here.
  • Fight the last plasma member and go east. Go up the stairs.
  • Set geodude to lead. Go east and fight N. Stealth rocks setup.
  • Joltik is Zoroark, so tri attack. Exit the cave.

MISTRALTON CITY & CELESTIAL TOWER

✓▾
  • Go north east from the pokecenter to route 7.
  • Go north through grass avoiding all trainers.
  • Go north avoiding the green haired trainer near the ramp.
  • Go north to Celestial Tower, avoiding trainers.
  • Go up the stairs. Avoid all trainers waiting for them to face away. Go up stairs. Go up stairs.
  • Click on the bell and say yes to ring it. Fly to Mistralton City.
  • Go north west all the way and go in the gym.
  • Cannon Sequence: Go north -> west -> east -> west -> north.
  • Follow the path and beat the gym leader with no setup.
  • Go south with the cannon and exit the gym. Heal at PC. Go north east to route 7.

ROUTE 7 & TWIST MOUNTAIN

✓▾
  • Get in 1 fight on route to level up.
  • Take first ramp. Take second ramp. Take north third ramp.
  • Put geodude at lead of party. Go north to fight Cheren.
  • Stealth Rocks lead into Nasty Plot. You will get a HM-Surf. Teach to bibarel.
  • Go north and enter the cave.
  • Follow the path and enter the door. Go east. Enter south door.
  • Avoid the hiker. Go east all the way. Go north. Enter the door.
  • Go north and enter the door. Go south and enter the door. Go south then west and enter the door.
  • IF WINTER: Go all the way north -> Enter door -> Go north to next area -> Go east through door to Icirrus City.
  • IF SPRING/SUMMER/AUTUMN: Go west -> Enter door -> Go north then east -> Enter door -> Go north -> Enter door -> Go north, fight hiker, enter door -> Go south avoiding martial artist -> Enter door -> Go east -> Enter door -> Go north to Cheren for cutscene -> Enter door.

ICIRRUS CITY

✓▾
  • Buy a silk scarf if you don’t have one and put it on Porygon.
  • Go in the gym north.
  • Ice Puzzle: Arrow (slide north) -> slide east -> red switch -> slide west -> slide east -> slide east -> slide north -> slide east -> slide north.
  • Step right to fight trainer, slide south. Slide east. Arrow (slide west) -> slide north to button -> slide north again.
  • Slide west -> arrow -> slide south -> west to arrow.
  • Slide north -> east -> north -> east -> north.
  • Go north and fight trainer. Step on button. Go east to arrow.
  • Slide south -> west -> south -> west -> north.
  • Swap item back to Chople berry.
  • Go fight the gym leader with nasty plot (If less than 50% HP, heal first).
  • Go on teleporter and exit the gym. Go north to Dragonspiral Tower.

DRAGONSPIRAL TOWER

✓▾
  • Go north to the next area. Enter the tower. Follow the path and go up the stairs.
  • Go west to the next area using strength to move boulder. Go up stairs. Jump south. Go all the way south and jump west 3 times. Jump north 1 time. Go up the stairs to the next area.
  • Follow the path into the next area, fighting team plasma members along the way.
  • Bridge Logic: East all way -> north bridge -> west all way -> north bridge.
  • Pick a stardust and go east to first bridge and go north.
  • East all way -> south bridge -> west all way -> south bridge.
  • Go east to the first bridge and go south on the bridge. Go west and go up the stairs to the next area.
  • Follow the path to team plasma members. Go up the stairs to the next area. Go north and talk to N.
  • Fly to Nimbasa City. Go south east to route 4.

ROUTE 4, DESERT & RELIC CASTLE

✓▾
  • Go south then, west and avoid all the trainers. Go north, follow the path and enter gate.
  • Go north all the way avoiding trainers. Go west until you see the castle entrance.
  • Relic Castle: Remember to walk over sand pits.
  • Avoid the trainers and go west all the way. Go down the stairs.
  • Go east to fight a plasma member. Go down in the sand.
  • Fight the plasma member. Go west all the way to fight a plasma member. Go down in the southern sand.
  • Fight plasma member. Go all the way west, fight plasma member. Go down in west sand pit by him.
  • Fight the next two plasma members. Go down sand pit. Cutscene.
  • Fly to Nacrene City.

NACRENE & OPELUCID

✓▾
  • Go north east to the gym and say yes to get a dark stone.
  • Fly to Icirrus City. Go east and go down the stairs. Go north to route 8.
  • Go north then east across water. Go south east to gate.
  • Put geodude in lead of party. Fight Bianca, use rocks, then nasty plot.
  • (if Meinfoo leads, hard swap to a sack to skip fake out, safe swap back for rocks).
  • Go east to tubeline bridge. Follow the path to route 9. Hug the south fence to avoid trainers. Go east to Opelucid City.
  • Go east and talk to Alder. Go talk to the girl in front of the house north west of the pokecenter and enter house.
  • Go north west and enter the gym.
  • Dragon Head Puzzle: Path then West (jump) -> North Head -> jump switch -> North Head -> jump switch -> South path -> West (jump) -> North Head -> jump switch -> North path to trainer -> South (jump) -> South Head -> jump switch -> South Head -> North path -> East path -> West (jump) -> North Head.
  • Beat the gym leader with nasty plot setup. Use the portal and exit the gym. Go north east to route 10.

ROUTE 10 & VICTORY ROAD

✓▾
  • Get in 1 fight on route to level before Cheren. Go north, avoid all trainers if possible.
  • At the bridge, put Geodude at lead of the party. Go east to fight Cheren, use rocks, then nasty plot.
  • Put Porygon back in lead. Continue east until gate and enter. Follow path.
  • In the area with the grass you can walk on the west side to only go on 2 grass squares.
  • In the area with the ice you have to slide north, east, north, west, north.
  • Victory Road: Enter door -> Up stairs east side -> South -> Enter door -> Slide down -> East -> Enter door -> Up west stairs -> Up north stairs -> Out south door -> Second door west -> Up stairs -> Up stairs NE -> Up stairs N -> Out south door to east -> Slide down right side -> Through door -> North to staircase -> Out south door -> Through gate to north.

POKEMON LEAGUE

✓▾
  • Make sure you have at least 4 Chople berries, a magnet, and some revives.
  • Log out and log back in if you get stuck in a bad spot (no lockout timer).
  • Psychic leader (top right): Magnet on Porygon (for Metagross), stealth rocks, and nasty plot.
  • Fighting leader (bottom right): Chople berry on Porygon, stealth rocks, and nasty plot.
  • (If Conkuldurr leads: rocks, sucker punch, then tri attack. Nasty plot on 2nd mon).
  • Dark and Ghost leaders (left side): Nasty plot with no rocks.
  • Beat the 4 trainers and then click on the statue to go down. Go north all the way and talk to N. Go north to N castle.

N CASTLE

✓▾
  • Equip Porygon with Chople berry. Go northwest then east. Go up the stairs.
  • Go east -> up stairs. Go west -> up stairs. Go east -> up stairs. Go west -> enter first room.
  • Go north and talk to N.
  • For Zekrom: tri attack, then shadow ball, then ultra ball until caught.
  • Beat N: Use dragon claw on Zekrom until dead, then geodude stealth rocks, then Porygon nasty plot setup.
  • (Note: First Archeops is Zoroark, use Tri Attack. If he shift gears into Klinklang before setup, Thunderbolt without setup).
  • Beat Ghetsis: Hard swap to Bibarel to go down, then into Porygon. Nasty plot setup, no stealth rocks.

POST-GAME & WATERFALL

✓▾
  • Nuvema: Go down stairs. Exit house. Go NE to Route 1. Go north and surf west. Go SW to Route 17.
  • Route 17: Surf west and go in the southern most current.
  • Route 18: Go up stairs -> follow path west -> Go down stairs west.
  • Go north, west, then south and pick up HM-Waterfall in the grass. Teach to Bibarel.
  • Fly to Nimbasa. Go NE and follow path to Black City. Go east then north through gate to Route 14.
  • Route 14: Go north into grass, surf water. Up waterfall, west, surf then down waterfall, west to Abundant Shrine.

THE END
