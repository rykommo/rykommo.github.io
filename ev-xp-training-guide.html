EV & XP Training

What are Effort Values (EVs)?

Effort Values (EVs) are bonus points that increase your Pokémon's stats beyond their natural growth. For every 4 EVs earned in a specific stat, that stat increases by 1 at Level 100.

In PokeMMO, you can earn a total of 510 EVs per Pokémon, with a maximum cap of 252 EVs in any single stat. Defeating Pokémon in hordes triggered by Sweet Scent is the fastest way to train.

Most of these Battles can and are advised to be cleared using a strong Zapdos as it has the hidden passive ability Coalescence. This gives Zapdos the ability to rarely (<1%) find an Elemental Orb upon defeating a Pokemon in any battle. Elemental Orbs are used to increase the IV stats of the legendary Bird Trio, so they are always in high demand, making them a nice passive income (usually they go for between 16k-21k on the GTL). If you have to clear hordes where electro moves wont work, you can use a special attack set Rampardos using Surf and the Moldbreaker ability to attack through Sturdy users or ground types.

XP Sharing

The XP Share is a held item that grants the holder a portion of the experience points and 100% of the EV's earned in battle, even if they never enter the field.

The EXP Reamplifier is a key item that shares experience and EV's across your entire party. This item is available in the RP shop for 1500RP and additionally to sharing XP across the Party also grants a 5% overall XP gain boost. If you have the money and want to build Raid Teams or Gym Rerun Teams, this item is very worth to buy and will save you alot of time.
