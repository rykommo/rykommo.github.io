Johto Story/Region Guide

NEW BARK TOWN

✓▾
  • Go down the stairs. Exit the house.
  • Go to the house north west of town. Talk to professor Elm.
  • Click on the machine north east to pick your starter. Exit the lab.
  • Go to the house north east. Talk to mom.
  • If you have another region completed here, talk again and go back to the previous region to grab pory and the team.
  • Exit the house. Go west to route 29.

ROUTE 29

✓▾
  • Go south west and follow the path until you see a guy with a blue hat.
  • Go north east a few steps and go north then west to Cherrygrove City.

CHERRYGROVE CITY

✓▾
  • Go a few steps west and you will get the running shoes.
  • Go into the PC and grab Pory and others.
  • Go north to route 30.

ROUTE 30

✓▾
  • Follow the path north.
  • Go north east all the way, skipping the first house.
  • Enter the house. Exit the house. TP.
  • Go south to Cherrygrove City. Go east to route 29 and you will fight your rival.

ROUTE 29

✓▾
  • Go east to New Bark Town.

NEW BARK TOWN

✓▾
  • Enter the house north west of the town.
  • Exit the house and TP or go west to Cherrygrove City.

ROUTE 30

✓▾
  • Go north west and fight the second guy with a blue hat while avoiding the first guy with a blue hat.
  • Go north to route 31 while avoiding all trainers.

ROUTE 31

✓▾
  • Follow the path and fight the trainer with the glasses near the cave.
  • Go west and enter the gate while avoiding all trainers.
  • Go west to Violet City.

VIOLET CITY

✓▾
  • Go north across the bridge and enter the Sprout Tower.

SPROUT TOWER

✓▾
  • Go north a few steps and go up the stairs.
  • Go east to fight a trainer. Go down the stairs.
  • Follow the path west to fight a trainer and go up the stairs.
  • Go south east and go up the stairs while avoiding the trainer.
  • Fight the next 2 trainers. Avoid the third trainer.
  • Fight the guy north west. Go north east, grab escape rope, and use it.

VIOLET CITY

✓▾
  • Go in the gym and avoid the 2 trainers using the glass bridge.
  • Beat the gym leader with no setup.
  • Go to pokemart and talk to the guy with glasses and take egg (make sure to have a spot in party).

VIOLET CITY

✓▾
  • Go south from the pokemart to route 32.

ROUTE 32

✓▾
  • Go south east. Go south until you see a guy with a blue hat. Avoid him.
  • Go south west through grass.
  • Continue south hugging the west wall to avoid the trainer.
  • Go down stairs and avoid a trainer with a blue hat by walking.
  • Fight the next trainer with a blue hat.
  • Go south from PC. Avoid the trainer. Enter the cave.

UNION CAVE

✓▾
  • Go west all the way to the wall. Go south and avoid the first hiker trainer.
  • Avoid the second hiker by walking.
  • Go south then west then south.
  • Go south east and avoid a trainer by walking. Exit the cave.

ROUTE 33

✓▾
  • Go west to Azalea Town.

AZALEA TOWN

✓▾
  • Go in the house north west of the town and talk to the old guy.
  • Exit the house and go in the cave north east of town.

SLOWPOKE WELL

✓▾
  • Go in the room west.
  • Go north and follow the path to fight 4 rocket trainers.

AZALEA TOWN

✓▾
  • Exit the house. Go in the gym.
  • Go north and enter the room.
  • Go on the western bug and fight the trainer.
  • Go north on the bug and fight the twins. Click on the blue switch.
  • Go on the bug. Click the red switch.
  • Go on the bug. Beat the gym leader with no setup.
  • Use teleporter. Exit the gym.
  • Go west from the pokecenter to fight your rival.
  • Go west to Ilex Forest.

ILEX FOREST

✓▾
  • Follow the path.
  • Go east and step on southern tree branches. Avoid other branches and click on the pokemon from behind.
  • Go east. Click on the pokemon from the south.
  • Go to the branches so that the pokemon looks east.
  • Go north a few steps and avoid the other branches. Click on the pokemon from the western side.
  • You will get the HM-Cut. Cut the tree.
  • Follow the path and avoid the first bug trainer by walking.
  • Fight the next trainer. Follow the path north. Avoid bug trainer by walking.
  • Go west to the gate.

ROUTE 34

✓▾
  • Go north while avoiding all the trainers by walking to Goldenrod City.

GOLDENROD CITY

✓▾
  • Enter the small house south east of pokemart and talk to the guy to get a bicycle.
  • Go to the radio tower building which is the black building north west from the pokecenter.
  • Talk to the third girl at the desk. Say yes to start a quiz.
  • Answers: Say yes, yes, no, no, no. You will get the radio card.
  • Exit the building and go to the gym north east.
  • Go up the stairs and go down the south stairs.
  • Go east all the way to the wall, then north through the gate. Fight 2 trainers.
  • Beat the gym leader with no setup, walk south to npc, and talk to gym leader.
  • Take the teleporter and exit the gym.
  • Go to the house next to the gym. Talk to the girl to get a squirt bottle.
  • Go north from the city to enter the gate.

ROUTE 35

✓▾
  • Avoid next 4 trainers. Follow the path and fight the 5th npc that you see.
  • Continue on path avoiding trainers.
  • Go east through grass and fight bug trainer.
  • Cut the tree and go north. Walk to avoid a trainer to the north east.

ROUTE 36

✓▾
  • Go east up stairs, avoiding trainer by walking.
  • Go east, avoid trainer, and use squirt bottle on Sudowoodo.
  • Go north.

ROUTE 37

✓▾
  • Go north west and avoid the bird trainer by walking. Avoid twins.
  • Go through the opening and fight the 2 girls trainers.
  • Go north to Ecruteak City.

ECRUTEAK CITY

✓▾
  • Go in the house north of the pokecenter. Fight the rocket and leave.
  • Go north west and enter Burned Tower.

BURNED TOWER

✓▾
  • Go north east and fight your rival with no setup.
  • Go down the stairs and go west to interact with beasts.
  • Go back to Ecruteak City.

ECRUTEAK CITY

✓▾
  • Go in the gym. Go north and fight the trainer.
  • Fall down or log out and log in again after trainer battle to get vision back.
  • Follow the path and fight the trainers.
  • Fight the gym leader with no setup. Fall down and exit the gym.
  • Go west from the city to route 38.

ROUTE 38

✓▾
  • Follow the road west past the first trainer. Fight the bird trainer watching path.
  • Jump ledge going west and then go north west avoiding trainers.

ROUTE 39

✓▾
  • Go south and avoid all the trainers except the last one watching path.
  • Go south to Olivine City.

OLIVINE CITY

✓▾
  • Go east from pokecenter and enter the Lighthouse.

LIGHTHOUSE

✓▾
  • Go north and go up the stairs.
  • Fight the 2 trainers and go up the stairs.
  • Avoid the next trainer by walking and go up the stairs.
  • Avoid the trainers and go in the door east.
  • Enter the door. Fight the trainer watching the stairs and go up the stairs.
  • Go up the stairs again.
  • Fight the sailor. Avoid the next trainer. Go up the stairs.
  • Talk to the girl. Use the elevator to exit the tower.
  • Go south west from the city to route 40.

ROUTE 40

✓▾
  • Surf south east.
  • Surf west as much as you can then south west to route 41 avoiding trainers.
  • Surf to Cianwood City.

CIANWOOD CITY

✓▾
  • Equip a Chople berry on Pory for this gym.
  • Go to the gym (pokemon are tough here recommend tri attack if none super effective).
  • Go north west avoiding the first trainer. Fight the next trainer.
  • Log out, log in, and click on the red valve to stop the water.
  • Beat the gym leader with Nasty Plot setup.
  • Exit the gym and you will get HM-Fly; teach it to Xatu if it doesn’t know it.
  • Go to the house west of the pokecenter and talk to the guy.
  • Fly to Olivine City.

OLIVINE CITY

✓▾
  • Go east from the pokemart and enter the Lighthouse.
  • Go in the elevator. Exit the elevator. Talk to the girl and say yes.
  • Use the elevator to exit the tower.
  • Go in the gym. Put geodude at the lead of the party.
  • Beat the leader with Stealth Rocks and Nasty Plot setup.
  • Exit the gym. Fly to Ecruteak City.
  • Go east to route 42.

ROUTE 42

✓▾
  • Avoid the trainer.
  • Go east and you will get the HM-Strength. Teach to Bibarel.
  • Surf south east avoiding the swimmer and fight the hiker trainer.
  • Continue east avoiding swimmer and fisher.
  • Use grass path to avoid next trainer then walk to avoid hiker. Avoid bird trainer.
  • Go to GTL. Buy 2 Magneton/zone around level 40 (or a magnemite and evo) with thunderbolt (~$2000-3000 each). Also can get magnemite and level (EXP Candy is about 5k).
  • Buy a level 1-10 who can learn surf (Poliwag is usually ~$1200) and one who can learn fly (Pidgey ~$1200). Put lvl 1 togepi in party.
  • Set team: Pory, 2 magneton/zone, Poliwag, Pidgey, and Togepi.
  • Put a Sitrus Berry on Porygon.
  • Go north to route 43.

ROUTE 43

✓▾
  • Go north west in the grass. Avoid trainers and follow the path north to the lake of rage.

LAKE OF RAGE

✓▾
  • Surf north west and fight the Red Gyarados.
  • Strategy: Lead with the 3 low level pokemon. There is a 28.5% damage cap but turn 1 is 7%.
  • Turn 1: Starts with all 3 low level mons in to get sacked. This is to skip the damage cap without taking damage.
  • Finish: When they die, put in Pory and the Magneton/zones and thunderbolt through the fight.
  • After the battle you will get the red scale.
  • Surf south a few steps and talk to the guy with the Dragonite.
  • If you want EXP Share: Fly to Cherrygrove City. Go north to route 30. Go north east all the way to the house and enter. Talk to the man to trade the red scale for the EXP Share.
  • Fly to Mahogany Town. Swap back to geodude and HM friends.
  • Go in the house north west from the pokecenter. Go down the stairs.

TEAM ROCKET HQ

✓▾
  • Go west all the way then south using the second path from the left for the trap floor so you only get 2 encounters and go down the stairs.
  • Go east then south through the opening then down stairs.
  • Go north west into the room to fight the pink haired trainer for password.
  • Go south west and fight the rocket trainer to get the second password.
  • Go north. Go east up the stairs.
  • Go west to fight the rocket. Go down the stairs.
  • Go south east. Click on the door to open it and fight the guy.
  • Beat him and go north west up the stairs. Go east all the way and go down the stairs.
  • Go south east all the way and go up the stairs. Go west then north through the door to fight the 2 trainers.
  • Fight the 3 Electrodes on the western side. Beat them and you will get the HM-Whirlpool.
  • Go south west and go up the stairs. Go in the yellow portal and exit the house.

MAHOGANY TOWN

✓▾
  • Go in the gym.
  • Slide north, west, north, east, north and enter the room.
  • Walk west and fight the girl in pink. Slide east into cube, slide east, north. Go through the door.
  • Walk west fight trainer. Go north then slide east into block. Slide back west and walk to position to slide north onto the block you just pushed. Slide east then north into the trainer. Slide west then north.
  • Beat the gym leader with Nasty Plot setup. Exit the gym using teleporter.
  • Fly to Goldenrod City.

GOLDENROD CITY

✓▾
  • Go in the house south west from the pokecenter. Go down the stairs.
  • Go north and talk to the rocket guy to put on a rocket uniform. Go up the stairs north west.
  • Exit the house. Go south west to the radio tower.

RADIO TOWER

✓▾
  • Go north east and talk to the rocket. Go upstairs.
  • Fight the first rocket girl trainer and avoid other trainers by walking. Go north west up the stairs.
  • Go up the north east stairs while avoiding the trainers.
  • Go up the north west stairs while avoiding the trainers.
  • Go south and fight the guy to get the basement key. Exit the building.

GOLDENROD CITY

✓▾
  • Go in the house south west from the pokecenter. Go down the stairs.
  • Go east and click on the door to open it. Go in the room. Go down the stairs.
  • Put geodude at the lead of your party. Go west to fight your rival with Stealth Rocks and Nasty Plot setup.
  • Continue west fighting the first rocket trainer.
  • Go south west and click on the blue switch, then green switch, then red switch.
  • Go south east and fight the trainer. Go through the door.
  • Follow the path avoiding the trainer to the south by hugging the left wall.
  • Continue along the path avoiding trainer. Fight the rocket north west.
  • Talk to the guy with the brown hat to get the card key.
  • Go north east and go up the stairs. (Amulet coin at top of stairs).
  • Go in the elevator and go to the 1F. Exit the building.
  • Go to the radio tower north west from the pokecenter.

RADIO TOWER

✓▾
  • Put a Silk Scarf on Porygon (saves some time compared to nasty plot setups for the next while).
  • Go north east and go up the stairs. Avoid trainers. Go north west up the stairs.
  • Fight the rocket south east. Click on the door to open it and fight a rocket. Go up the stairs.
  • Fight the guy and go up the stairs. Fight the trainer.
  • Go in the elevator and exit. Go north to fight the guy. Go in the elevator and exit.
  • Go down the stairs and exit the building.
  • Fly to Mahogany Town. Go east to route 44.

ROUTE 44

✓▾
  • Go east avoiding the trainers by walking until the bridge then hug the south wall to avoid.
  • Stop when you see the hiker watching the path. Lead with Geodude Stealth Rocks setup for the hiker.
  • Go east and fight the last trainer before the cave. Enter the cave.

ICE PATH

✓▾
  • Follow the path until you get to the floor with ice and rocks on it.
  • Slide: North, west, north, west, south, west, north, east.
  • Next ice patch slide: East, north, west, south, west, north, east. Pickup HM-Waterfall.
  • Slide: West, south. Continue along the path down the ladder.
  • Follow the path and push the first boulder that you see 4 times north.
  • Push the second boulder that you see 1 time east, then 3 times south, then 5 times west, 1 time south, and 1 time east.
  • Go south east all the way and go near the easternmost boulder.
  • Push it: 1 time west, 5 times south, 1 time west, 3 times south, 1 time north, 4 times west.
  • Push the next boulder: north 2 times, 2 times east, 3 times north, 2 times west.
  • Push the last boulder: east 1 time and south 1 time.
  • Go north east and go down the ladder. Walk 1 step west. Walk 2 steps north.
  • Slide: West, south, west, north. Go down the stairs.
  • Go east up the ladder. Go south and slide to the ladder and go up.
  • Go west up the ladder. Stand on the easternmost tile before the ice floor.
  • Slide: South, west, south, east. Talk to the girl. Slide south. Exit the cave.

BLACKTHORN CITY

✓▾
  • Make sure to have a whirlpool user in the team. Go in the gym north of the city.
  • Puzzle 1: Left arrow, button 2 times, right arrow. Avoid the first trainer by walking. Fight the next trainer.
  • Puzzle 2: Up arrow, the button, right arrow, button 2 times, right arrow. Fight the next trainer then avoid the next two by walking.
  • Puzzle 3: Left arrow, button 2 times, right arrow, button, up arrow 3 times, the button, right arrow, button, up arrow 2 times.
  • Beat the gym leader with Nasty Plot setup. Go on the teleporter.
  • Exit the gym and go north. Enter the dragon's den cave.

DRAGON'S DEN

✓▾
  • Go down the stairs. Fight the trainer. Go south and surf south west.
  • Whirlpool across and enter house.
  • Answers: Say ally or friend. Say strategy or training. Say Strong or anyone. Say love or knowledge. Say both.
  • Exit the house. Surf north west and use whirlpool. Exit the cave.
  • Fly to New Bark Town.

NEW BARK TOWN

✓▾
  • Go in the house north west. You will get a master ball. Exit the house.
  • Fly to Ecruteak City.

ECRUTEAK CITY

✓▾
  • Put a Magnet on Porygon (for Vaporeon). Go in the house north from the pokecenter. Say yes to fight the trainers.
  • Go to GTL and buy the cheapest 4 to fill out the team: Level 40+ Golducks - cloud nine ability, hydro pump, and below level cap of level 48.
  • Keep 1 fly user for after the fight. Otherwise, you can re-enter the tower, get an encounter, and disconnect which will reset you to PC.
  • Go to the temple north east. Talk to the guy east and go down the stairs. Go north and go up the stairs.

BELLCHIME TRAIL

✓▾
  • Go east and enter the tower.

BELL TOWER

✓▾
  • Go north west and go up the stairs. Go south and go up stairs.
  • Ramps: Go north east ramp. West ramps all the way. East ramps to stairs. Go west ramp and follow to stairs.
  • Jump path: Go east then first southern ramp. Jump south following left on first split, then right path on second split. Go up stairs.
  • Go up stairs again: Follow the path towards the south west and go up stairs.
  • Portals: Skip first teleporter. Follow path until next teleporter. Take next teleporter. Take another teleporter. Take last teleporter.
  • Go up stairs twice.
  • Ho-Oh: 7% damage cap on turn 1. So start with fly user to save the Golducks.
  • Strategy: Don’t nasty plot, 28.5% HP cap. Attack Ho-oh with hydro pump and thunderbolt, ignore beasts.
  • Fly to New Bark Town. Go east to route 27.

ROUTE 27

✓▾
  • Go east until you see a cave entrance. Go in the cave.

TOHJO FALLS

✓▾
  • Go up the waterfall and go down the next waterfall. Go south east and exit the cave.

ROUTE 27

✓▾
  • Avoid the trainer in red. Start walking then surf east and avoid the next trainers.
  • Continue east avoiding trainers to route 26.

ROUTE 26

✓▾
  • Follow the path north to the victory road.

VICTORY ROAD

✓▾
  • Push boulder north. Follow path and when you need to pick a direction go west.
  • Go north west and go up the stairs. Go south then follow path to ladder and go up. Fall down hole.
  • Go north west, push boulder, go up the ladder. Go south and follow the path while avoiding the hole.
  • Go south west and follow the path. Go in either hole. Go up the ladder.
  • Follow path and when you need to pick a direction, put geodude at lead of party and go east.
  • Fight your rival with rocks and nasty plot setup. Go north to the Indigo Plateau.

INDIGO PLATEAU

✓▾
  • Make sure to have potions and revives ready. Go north and enter the building.
  • Go north all the way and talk to the guard. Enter the room.
  • Prep: I recommend buying a level 50-55 starmie (or staryu and evo) that has psychic for Bruno.
  • Will (Psychic): Rocks/Nasty Plot Setup (Espeon doesn’t have magic bounce).
  • Koga (Poison): Rocks/Nasty Plot Setup.
  • Bruno (Fighting): Highest success rate strategy: Make sure to have Chople Berry equipped. Machamp is the main issue.
  • Bruno Strategy: If Machamp leads, get starmie in before Pory and use psychic. Use tri attack until you see Steelix, then Nasty plot before attacking. Otherwise just skip Nasty Plot all together.
  • Warning: He has a lot of priority moves; have revives/potions ready. Revive and potion to 100% before bringing Porygon back.
  • Karen (Dark): Nasty Plot Setup.
  • Champion Lance: Use Sitrus Berry on Porygon. Rocks/Nasty Plot Setup. You might go down but just revive and come back full hp.
  • Ttar Logic: If Ttar comes out before setup, throw out Golduck to waste a turn of sandstorm and surf.

NEW BARK TOWN

✓▾
  • Enter the door and you will get a s.s.ticket. Say red and exit the house.
  • Exit the laboratory and enter the house north east/west (depending on gender).
  • Talk to your mom 2 times to get a amuletcoin.
  • THE END!
