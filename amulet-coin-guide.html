Amulet Coin Farming

Efficient Pickpocket Strategy at Island One.

Why Farm Amulet Coins?

  • »
    Market Value: Sells for 19,000 - 22,000 average.
  • »
    Hourly Yield: Approximately 150,000 - 200,000 per hour.
  • »
    Utility: Increases money from trainers/gyms by 50%.
  • »
    Demand: High resale due to consumable status.
  • »
    Shiny Chance: High Wild Encounter rates for Shiny Payday/Pickup Pokémon.

🧭 Location & Method

The Spot

Island One (Kanto). Surf right of Poké Center, head North into the grass.

1

Use Sweet Scent to attract a horde. Run from Ponyta's since they have nothing of value.

2

Use Imprison to block meowth from using Hypnosis/Nasty Plot. If the quick claw item on a meowth lets it move first here you can run since its not worth to try and steal the quick claw.

3

Switch to Weavile to steal the item on contact. Sometimes you may see the Frisk ability pop up briefly for a second time when the battle starts, if this happened attack with Gothitelle's thief until you steal one of the items and the do then switch

When you run out of sweet scent pp either refresh pp using leppa berries if you dont mind loosing a bit of profit or head north into the cave and heal up in the middle of the hot spring (go into the middle of the big pool and press A to heal your pokemon).

Required Squad Builds

Why Gothitelle?

  • • Imprison disables wild Meowth's non-damaging moves.
  • • Forces Meowth into contact-only moves.
  • • Bulky and outspeeds wild spawns.
  • • Has Frisk to detect Quick claws or Amulet Coins.
